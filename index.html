Technology, Media & Finance

Some of the Companies George Has Built

Boston Technologies

Boston Technologies (BT) was a technology, market-making, and high-frequency trading company in FX, precious metals, and CFDs. George founded and bootstrapped BT from $0 to $20M+ in revenue, earning multiple years on the Inc. 500/5000 list before exiting the company in 2014.

Lampix & the PIX Platform

Lampix is a table-top augmented reality company in New York that George co-founded and led as CEO. Lampix won "Best AR/VR Startup" at SXSW and raised 40,912 ETH to build the PIX platform, a decentralized human-in-the-loop system for image sourcing, labeling, and detection.

Media, Investing & Advisory

George founded Lending Times, an award-winning media company in the online lending space, and Blockchain Times. He has been a partner at LunaCap Ventures and an advisor to multiple blockchain and fintech companies, alongside his real estate and private fixed income investing.
